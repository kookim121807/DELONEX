Partnerships

DELONEX seeks global partnerships, sponsors, and collaborators. Any company is welcome to collaborate with us, though we will accept at most five collaborators in total. We are especially eager to work with institutions related to mathematics, physics, AI, machine learning, and other STEM fields. Both nonprofit and for-profit organizations are welcome, with a slight preference for for-profit partners.

If you are interested in partnering with us, sponsoring us, or collaborating with us, please fill out the partnership form below. We will select up to five partners, sponsors, or collaborators. We are willing to offer 2% equity to each selected organization.
